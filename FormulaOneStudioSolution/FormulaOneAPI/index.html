Project description

There are moments that will remain forever etched in the minds of each of us, chills that will mark our lives forever, names that are myths, legends and that will be forever, because the history of a people, a nation or even the world whole is written through sport, through overtaking or memorable accidents because a man's life can radically change in 120 minutes of racing, because Formula 1 can crown you or even take your life.

It is difficult to tell a story so full of emotions, a story lived at three hundred an hour. Certainly one cannot fail to mention some names of legends, drivers who have won a lot, who have won everything and others who have disappeared too early whose numbers are not enough to describe their size.

Since dawn, since Formula 1 was a very dangerous event and every corner could be fatal, we must remember Tazio Nuvolari, immediately the immortal Enzo Ferrari had glimpsed something in him, something that he would only find in Gilles Villeneuve, who he would still have great reserved show if only he had lived a little longer, the list would be very long, timeless names like: Mansell, Prost, Fangio, Moss, Ascari, Lauda, ​​Piquet, Stewart or Clark but also pilots who still race in Formula 1 as Hamilton or Vettel. Then there are two immortal names, it is not known who is the greatest but both will be impressed as well as in the history books, in the mind of each of us: Schumaker and Senna. In fact, to tell a story, names are used and then places, those places that represent memory but which are also a launching pad for the future, places where champions are crowned, one especially Montecarlo, one of the most popular circuits. Insidious of the season but with a panorama of the only port in the world and then there are two tracks that count the most races held: Monza and Silverstone.

In addition to the names and places there are the numbers: 7, like the championships won by the greatest ever: Schumaker, nobody like him, or 6: the number that Nico Rosberg brought to the car when he was crowned world champion in 2016 or 100 podiums conquered by Kimi Raikkonen ... and this list could continue indefinitely, infinite, like the fourth point of this story or emotions: the adrenaline, the wait, the silence that pervades the body for a moment before the lights go out, this is the essence of Formula 1: work, patience, disappointments, but also pride, a checkered flag and victory!
